Voting Policy

Read the guidelines to ensure a smooth and transparent voting process.

Overview

The voting process is designed to ensure fairness and transparency in selecting the King and Queen for each academic major. As a student, you are allowed to vote for one King and one Queen from your respective major. This policy helps maintain the integrity of the voting process.

Eligibility Requirements

Only students who are officially enrolled in the respective academic major are eligible to vote. If you are unsure about whether you're eligible, please refer to the student registration office or contact our support team.

  • You must be an active student of the university.
  • You must be registered in one of the participating majors.
  • Each student is allowed one vote for King and one vote for Queen within their respective major.

Voting Process

The voting process is simple and straightforward. Below are the steps you need to follow:

  1. Login to your student portal with your university credentials.
  2. Navigate to the “Vote Now” section.
  3. Select your King and Queen candidates from the list of nominees in your major.
  4. Click “Submit” to cast your vote.
  5. Once submitted, your vote is final and cannot be changed.

Voting Rules

  • One vote per student: You may cast one vote for the King and one vote for the Queen only.
  • Vote within your major: Make sure to vote for contestants within your own academic major.
  • Confidentiality and fairness: Your vote is confidential, and each student can only vote for one title (King and Queen).
  • Rule violations: Any violations of these rules may lead to disqualification or other disciplinary actions.

Voting Period

The voting period will begin on January 10, 2024 and will close on January 20, 2024. Make sure to vote within this timeframe!

A: If you are currently enrolled in a participating major, you are eligible to vote. If you're unsure, please contact the student registration office.
